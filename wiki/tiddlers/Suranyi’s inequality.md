## Statement of the inequality

Let $a_1 , a_2 , \dots,a_n$ be non-negative real
numbers. Then
$$(n-1)\sum_{i=1}^n a_i^n + n\prod_{i=1}^n a_i\ge \left(\sum_{i=1}^n a_i\right)\left(\sum_{i=1}^n a_i^{n-1} \right) $$
